About the Artist

Domiel May R. Mercado’s journey is a captivating blend of grace, imagination, and artistry that transcends her roles as a model and brand ambassador. Each chapter in her life unfolds through her artistic expression, revealing the layers of her creative soul.

Nature, dreams, and the joys of motherhood form the thematic heart of Domiel’s work. Through vibrant hues and delicate compositions, her brush captures the beauty of flora, fauna, and ecosystems. Each canvas invites viewers into landscapes where the natural world comes alive with color and movement.

Her unique style of non-representational and distorted realism allows emotions to guide her compositions. By moving beyond literal representations, she crafts a visual language that resonates on a deep, emotional level. Her art transforms intangible feelings into expressive, abstract forms.

Domiel’s dream-inspired pieces are both surreal and ethereal, blending reality with imagination. Through abstract expressions, she translates her inner visions, inviting viewers to traverse between the realms of reality and fantasy.

Motherhood infuses her work with a personal, heartfelt dimension. Each piece is a celebration of life and family, capturing the nuances of maternal bonds and the transformative experiences of raising children. Through her art, motherhood emerges as a profound, enduring theme.

Currently residing in the peaceful setting of the University of the Philippines - Los Baños Faculty Village, Domiel continues to evolve artistically. Her work is a testament to her harmonious fusion of nature, dreams, motherhood, and abstract realism, inviting viewers to explore the boundless realms of her creativity.
